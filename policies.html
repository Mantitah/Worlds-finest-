Shipping & Returns

Estimated shipping arrival

We typically process orders within 1–3 business days. Shipping transit times vary by carrier and location, but most domestic orders arrive within 2–7 business days after shipment. In total, expect delivery within approximately 7 business days from order placement in most cases.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. At this time we do not offer exchanges — returns are issued as store credit which can be applied to future purchases.

How to initiate a return

  1. Contact us at support@worldsfinest.example with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will provide instructions and a return authorization.
  3. Once the item is received and inspected, we will issue store credit to your account.

If you have any questions about shipping, returns, or your order, reply to your order confirmation email or contact support.
